The Energy Carbon Footprint service
===============================

This service offers to offset the carbon footprint for the given energy comsumption

Objective
---------
/packet_size (ethereum_common/UInt256 from robonomics_comm metapackage) - the amount of consumed energy that the service offers to offset, Watt*hour

Result
------
/packet_size (ethereum_common/UInt256 from robonomics_comm metapackage) - the amount of energy that the service offsets for the carbon footprint, Watt*hour
/stamp (ethereum_common/UInt256 from robonomics_comm metapackage) - the timestamp of result publication, epoch time in seconds
